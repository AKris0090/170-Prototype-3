MY FAVORITE POEMS

1.

In a hall, I stand alone,
No voice, no breath, I erode but remain,
I know my nature,
But what is my make.

2.

From my window I sit with grace,
Overlooking play, overlooking chase,
Bright colours but a plain vase

3.

On the slopes I’m worn
Without me in a fall you’ll be torn,
Asunder,
Got your board on your back but don’t forget me,
Buster,

4.

Above the ice, I catch your eye,
The first in line, as skaters glide by.
Not too high, but hard to miss,
A lucky number in a frosty abyss.
What am I?
